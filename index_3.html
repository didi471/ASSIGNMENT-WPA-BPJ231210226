How to take a Nutrition ?

  • Healthy Snacks: Encourage light foods like nuts, seeds, fruit and yogurt instead of processed snacks. This option is more nutritious and helps maintain their energy levels
  • Limit Sugary Foods: Reduce intake of sugary drinks and snacks such as snacks and carbonated water. Instead, choose water, milk or natural fruit juice
  • Regular Meals: Make sure they eat regularly and avoid skipping breakfast. A good breakfast can improve concentration and performance at school
